Welcome to the Vivid Voltage Pack Opener!
This app is inspired by MaxMoeFoe's Pokemon series, and allows you to open a virtual Vivid Voltage Booster Pack.

To make things a bit more interesting, seeding rates have been adjusted.

Rough odds of pulling at least one per pack of 10:

Rarity Odds
Common 100%
Uncommon 99%
Rare 80%
Rare Holo 10%
Ultra Rare 5%
Amazing Rare 1%
Secret Rare / Rainbow Rare 5%
